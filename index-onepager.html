Who We Are

The Oxford Fintech & Legaltech Society (previously "Fintech & SmartLaw Society", FSS) was founded in 2017 by a group of Masterâ€™s students in Law & Finance in University of Oxford to discuss the impact modern technology is having on banks, financial institutions, legal services, and regulations.

The Society aims to foster and develop education, research, community, and innovation in the field of financial and legal technology. Throughout the year, the committee organises various events and opportunities in the University for our members - which includes students, researchers, alumni, industry professionals, sponsors - and the public.

Our past events have included smart contracts workshops and panel discussions with academics and industry professionals in fintech, legaltech, and blockchain. We also provide opportunities for aspiring students to meet and network with experts and alumni, and help prepare for job interviews in the corresponding field. We have recently taken research initiatives to look explore the impact of blockchain technology on financial regulations, and provide guidance on cases related to Initial Coin Offerings.
